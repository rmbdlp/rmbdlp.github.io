Away From Keyboard

Micro Apparel Brand

I started a micro apparel brand to encourage individuals to disconnect from our devices and to engage with more meaningful interactions and with the "now". I ran a campaign where the proceed went directly towards "Save the Children" to support the effects of Typhoon Mangkhut. I work with suppliers overseas to negotiate the best prices without sacrificing quality.
